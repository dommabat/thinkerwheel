Our Mission

Every inch matters.

Thinker Wheel believes that every effort toward supporting family education brings equality, morality, and security to society. We work where it matters most — inside the family — because change that starts at home radiates outward into communities and across generations.


Our Vision

We believe education empowers every individual — from earning a livelihood to understanding healthcare, rights, and civic life. We are here to bridge the family education gap across Mongolia, focusing on women's empowerment, parenting, and early childhood learning.

Priority Areas

Where we focus
our energy.

Women Empowerment

Supporting women to lead with confidence — within their families, communities, and beyond — through knowledge, skills, and solidarity.

Parenting

Equipping parents with practical tools, resources, and a supportive community to raise thriving children in modern Mongolia.

Early Education

Investing in the earliest years of a child's life — the foundation upon which all future learning and flourishing is built.

Publishing · HarperCollins Partnership
01

Kids' Book Translation

In partnership with HarperCollins, we translated and published beloved children's books — including the NY Times bestselling series by Jory John & Pete Oswald — into Mongolian. The editions have been donated to kindergartens across Ulaanbaatar and are now on shelves in major bookstores nationwide, putting quality literature in every child's hands.

Advocacy · Community Health
02

Clean Air Near Schools

We fundraised and ran a public awareness campaign addressing diesel fuel emissions around school areas in Ulaanbaatar. By collaborating with kindergartens and local organizations, we reached communities through social media and community events — protecting the air where children learn and play.
